Privacy Policy

RPM Pro · Last updated: March 2026

RPM Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mileage, expense, and tax-reporting app for owner-operators and small fleets. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data we collect

  • Account information (when you sign up): Email address and a hashed password. We do not store your plain-text password.
  • App data you create: Trips (miles, rates, dates, notes), expenses (amounts, categories, dates, notes), and app settings (e.g. default rate per mile, language preference).
  • Receipt photos (optional, Premium): If you use the receipt feature, photos are taken or chosen by you and stored only on your device (and, if you use cloud sync, in your own synced data—see below). We do not upload receipt images to our own servers for advertising or analytics.
  • Subscription status: When you subscribe to Premium, the app uses RevenueCat and the Apple App Store or Google Play to process payments. We do not collect or store your payment card details; Apple and Google handle that. We only receive information that you have an active Premium subscription so the app can unlock Premium features.

2. How we use your data

  • To run the app: Your trips, expenses, and settings are used to show your dashboard, summaries, and exports (PDF, CSV) that you choose to generate and share.
  • Cloud sync (optional): If you create an account, we sync your app data (trips, expenses, settings) to our cloud provider (Supabase) so you can use the same data on other devices when you sign in. Sync is tied to your account and is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.
  • Subscriptions: RevenueCat and the stores (Apple/Google) use data necessary to process and restore purchases. Their privacy policies apply to that processing.

3. Data storage and retention

  • On your device: App data and preferences are stored locally. Receipt images you add are stored in the app's local storage on the device.
  • In the cloud: If you use an account, your synced data is stored with Supabase (our cloud provider) and is retained until you delete your account or ask us to delete your data.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers that help us run the app (e.g. Supabase for sync, RevenueCat/Apple/Google for subscriptions), under contracts that limit use to providing the service.
  • If required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

5. Your choices

  • You can use the app without an account (local-only mode); in that case, data stays on your device.
  • You can sign out or delete your account; we can delete your synced data upon request.
  • You can disable camera or photo access in your device settings; the app will work without receipt photos.
  • You can export or delete your data using the in-app Backup & Restore and Reset options in Settings.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices (e.g. HTTPS, secure auth and storage) to protect your data. No system is completely secure; we encourage strong passwords and keeping your device secure.

7. Children

RPM Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to request deletion of your data, contact us at: Rpmpro.support@gmail.com
